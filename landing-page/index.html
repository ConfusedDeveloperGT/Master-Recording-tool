Powerful Capabilities

Everything you need for seamless audio control.

Real-time Live Listen

Stream high-fidelity PCM audio directly from the device's microphone to your web dashboard with sub-second latency.

Remote Recording

Trigger background recording sessions remotely. Files are saved in high-quality AAC format and automatically uploaded to the server.

How it Works

Get up and running in minutes.

01

Install Mobile App

Download the MicNet APK/IPA on your target device and launch the app. Grant microphone permissions.

02

Pair the Device

Open the MicNet Web Dashboard and scan the QR code with the mobile app to securely link them.

03

Listen & Record

Monitor your devices from anywhere. Tap "Live Listen" to hear what's happening or trigger remote recordings.
